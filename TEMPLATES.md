## Next Templates (Priority Order)

### 1. Villa/Property Rental — "Tropica" 🏠
**Target client:** Villa owners, property managers in Bali/SE Asia
**Why #1:** 50,000+ villas in Bali, most need direct booking sites to avoid 15-20% Airbnb fees.
**Monthly charge:** Rp 5-10M ($300-625)

**Business sections:**
- Hero with stunning property photos (full-bleed gallery)
- Property listings with search/filter (location, bedrooms, price range, amenities)
- Property detail: photo gallery (lightbox), amenity icons, floor plan, map, seasonal pricing table
- Availability calendar with date range picker
- Booking/inquiry form → WhatsApp notification to owner + email confirmation to guest
- Guest reviews/testimonials
- About/team section (property manager profile)
- Blog/travel guide (SEO content)

**Admin panel:**
- Property management (CRUD with multi-image upload)
- Booking calendar (see all bookings across properties)
- Seasonal pricing editor (peak/off-peak/holiday rates)
- Guest management (contact history)
- Revenue dashboard (occupancy rate, monthly revenue)
- Block dates (maintenance, personal use)

**Key differentiator:** Direct booking = no OTA commission. Pays for itself in 1-2 bookings.

### 2. Medical/Dental Clinic — "Medica" ⚕️
**Target client:** Dentists, aesthetic clinics, specialist doctors, wellness clinics
**Why:** Medical tourism $10B+ in SE Asia. Single patient worth Rp 1-50M.
**Monthly charge:** Rp 3-7M ($190-440)

**Business sections:**
- Hero with clinic interior/team photo
- Services catalog (treatments with descriptions, duration, price ranges)
- Doctor/practitioner profiles (photo, qualifications, specializations, languages)
- Online appointment booking (pick service → pick doctor → pick date/time)
- Before/after gallery (for aesthetic/dental)
- Patient testimonials with star ratings
- Insurance/payment info section
- Location with map + operating hours
- FAQ section
- Blog/health articles (SEO)

**Admin panel:**
- Service management (CRUD)
- Doctor/staff management
- Appointment calendar (daily/weekly view)
- Patient inquiries list
- Before/after photo management
- Operating hours editor

**Key differentiator:** Professional medical website builds trust. Patients choose clinics based on website quality.

**Admin panel:**
- Practice area management
- Lawyer profile management

**Key differentiator:** Trust-building design. Law firms pay premium for professional, credible online presence.

### 4. Architecture / Interior Design — "Forma" 🏗️
**Target client:** Architecture firms, interior designers, landscape architects, contractors
**Why:** Portfolio-driven business. Website IS the showroom. Bali/Jakarta building boom.
**Monthly charge:** Rp 3-7M ($190-440)

**Business sections:**
- Hero with full-bleed project photography
- Project portfolio (filterable: residential, commercial, hospitality, landscape)
- Project detail: large photo gallery, project specs (area, budget range, duration), description, 3D renders
- Services (design, build, renovate, consult)
- Team/studio section (architect profiles, firm story)
- Process section (how we work: consult → design → build → handover)
- Awards/publications
- Contact/consultation booking

**Admin panel:**
- Project portfolio management (CRUD with multi-image, categories)
- Service package management
- Team member management
- Inquiry/lead management
- Image gallery management with drag-reorder

**Key differentiator:** Architecture firms live and die by their portfolio presentation. Stunning photography display = winning projects.

## Template Design Principles

1. **Every template has an admin panel** — business owner manages their content
2. **Real Unsplash images** — full visual experience out of the box
3. **Multiple theme variants** — same business, different aesthetic
4. **Mobile-first** — most visitors browse on phone
5. **WhatsApp as primary CTA** — SE Asian market standard
6. **Booking/inquiry built-in** — not an "integration," it's a business section
7. **IDR + USD pricing** — dual market (local + expat/tourist)
8. **SEO-ready** — metadata, structured data, blog capability
